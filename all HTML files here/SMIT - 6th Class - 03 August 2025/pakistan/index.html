Pakistan

Pakistan is a country located in South Asia, sharing borders with India, Afghanistan, Iran, and China. It also has a coastline along the Arabian Sea. With a population exceeding 240 million people, it is the fifth most populous country in the world. The country was formed in 1947 after gaining independence from British rule and was created as a homeland for Muslims from the Indian subcontinent. Pakistan has four provinces: Punjab, Sindh, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (KPK), and Balochistan, along with federal territories like Islamabad and Gilgit-Baltistan. The capital city is Islamabad, while Karachi is the largest and most economically significant city. Pakistan has a rich cultural heritage, diverse ethnic groups, and numerous languages spoken throughout the nation, with Urdu being the national language and English widely used for official purposes. The country has mountains, deserts, plains, and rivers. It is home to the world's second-highest peak, K2, and many ancient civilizations like the Indus Valley. Agriculture, textiles, sports goods, and information technology play key roles in its economy. Pakistanis are known for their hospitality, resilience, and strong family values. Despite challenges such as political instability and economic issues, Pakistan continues to strive for development and global recognition.
